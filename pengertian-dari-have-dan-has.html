19. jelaskan perbedaan dan pengertian was/were, dan has/have ,kasih contoh


Untuk mengetahui perbedaan penggunaan was / were dan has / have, kita perlu mengetahui konteks kalimatnya.

Pembahasan was / were sebagai bentuk past tense dan subjunctive verb :

was / were digunakan sebagai auxiliary verbs dalam bentuk past tense, past continuous tense, and past passive tense

A. Auxiliary was digunakan jika subyek-nya tunggal.

Contoh :

I was really scared after watching a horror movie last night.She was happy to celebrate her birthday.It was left for several days before the owner came to take it.

B. Auxiliary were digunakan jika subyek-nya jamak.

Contoh :

You were brave enough to reach home alone by yourself.We were carrying these boxes together.  If I were you, I would call him soon. They were really hopeful about the announcement.has / have sebagai bentuk present perfect tense dan present perfect continuous tense

A. Have / has dipakai sebagai kata kerja (menunjukkan kepemilikan)

Jika subjek-nya orang ketiga tunggal, maka gunakan has

Jika subjek-nya orang pertama, orang kedua, dan orang ketiga jamak, maka gunakan have

Contoh :

I have many stories to tell you. You have some pencils, don't you ?We have two balloons left.They have some breads.She has something in her mind.He has many cakes in his fridge

B. Have / has dipakai sebagai auxiliary verb dalam bentuk present perfect tense dan present perfect continuous tense

Jika subjek-nya orang ketiga tunggal, maka gunakan has

Jika subjek-nya orang pertama, orang kedua, dan orang ketiga jamak, maka gunakan have

Contoh :

I have waited for you here.  She has talked to her teacher about her yesterday absence.You haven't finished your work yet.We have slept for five hours.She has been living in this country for many years. It has been living in this house for decades.
